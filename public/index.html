Marathon

What is Marathon?

Marathon is a service that intends to accelerate and simplify the fitness integration process. Developers can now easily integrate fitness-tracking into their applications without hassle.

Made By Developers...
For Developers...

How does Marathon work?

Marathon connects many health related services into one platform to allow for an all-for-one easy access tool.

What does Marathon Support?

The Marathon team is working hard to get support from as many health related services as possible.

Marathon is not sponsored by or affiliated with Fitbit and/or Garmin
